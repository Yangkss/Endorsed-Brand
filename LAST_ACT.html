What is Nike?

Nike is a global leader in athletic footwear, apparel, and sports equipment. Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports and officially becoming Nike, Inc. in 1971, the company is known for its iconic Swoosh logo and "Just Do It" slogan.

Nike designs innovative products for athletes of all levels and sponsors some of the world's top sports teams and figures, making it a powerhouse in both performance and lifestyle fashion.
